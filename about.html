Carlos Avila: Sculptor of Sound

For over two decades, Carlos Avila has been a dedicated creative partner to the independent music community. As the Owner and Head Engineer of Studio Resonance MTL, he approaches audio not just as a technical craft, but as an art form.

Carlos's philosophy is simple: create a sanctuary of inspiration. He strips away the pressure, fostering a warm, collaborative space where artists can freely harness their raw creative energy and reach their true potential.

He specializes in unconventional recording and sonic experimentation, thriving on bringing complex artistic ideas to life. If the music is alive in your mind, Carlos will refine that inspiration and transform it into an undeniable, resonant reality.
